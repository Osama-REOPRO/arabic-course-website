# Vowels and how they are written
In each lesson a small section will be devoted to the arabic alphabet, and for the first few lessons we are only going to be practicing the 3 vowel letters, that's it, only those 3, we want to master them first and then this knowledge will transfer beautifully to the rest of the alphabet
So we learn how Alif, waw and yaa are written in the beginnin, middle and end of the sentence, we look at the words we've learned today and we just try to find those letters and notice how they are written.
We just try and identify them
## exercise
I write a word all the way until it is time to write one of the vowels and then I stop and ask one of the students which version of the letter should I be writing now? (beginning, middle, end version)
