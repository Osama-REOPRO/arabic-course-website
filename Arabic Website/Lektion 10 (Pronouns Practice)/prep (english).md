# What is this "ya"?
this is used to specify who we are talking to, if you want to mention the person you are talking with by name then you put "ya" before it, it is sort of like how you say: "I pray to you, O god" it is like that "O" (find an example in german)
At the end of any question you ask, you can always add "ya+name" to the end to kind specify who specifically you are speaking with
# What is "tayyeb"?
when someone answers a question with "no", and you want to ask them back "then who/what?", tayyeb is the equivalent of "then" (find equivalent in german)
